Exam Results

Our examination results are excellent, with our students achieving grades that compete with many of the top schools across the world. Considering that we are a non selective, responsibility inclusive school, these results are even more impressive.

2022

A Level:

The cohort of our 75 students entering for the A Level qualification achieved the following results:

The pass rate was 98%

  • A*: 10%
  • A*-A: 29%
  • A*-B: 62%
  • A*-C: 83%

AS Level:

The cohort of our 99 students entering for the AS Level qualification achieved the following results:

  • A: 42%
  • A-B: 66%
  • A-C: 81%
  • A*D: 96%

GCSE:

The cohort of our 101 students entering for the GCSE qualification achieved the following results:

The pass rate was 100%

  • 9: 16%

  • 9-6:74%
  • 9-4:98%

BTEC:

The cohort of our 12 students entering for Level 3 BTEC qualification achieved the following results:

The pass rate was 100%

  • Pass: 14%
  • Merit: 5%
  • Distinction: 24%
  • Distinction*: 57%

  • The University of Manchester
