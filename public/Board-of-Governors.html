Board of Governors

The English College is governed by a Board of Governors who represent the best interests of the students, teachers, staff and overall school community. They also provide governance to the school on policies, compliance and financial matters.

Mark Ford

PRINCIPAL

Mark Ford brings a significant amount of experience and expertise to The English College. Mark has worked in both UK state and independent schools, but the majority of his experience has been in international schools across the world, in Europe, Africa, the Middle East and South East Asia. Mark is passionate about teaching, learning and helping students, teachers and leaders to be the best that they can be.

Maud Forest-Perrot

HR & ADMINISTRATION DIRECTOR

Maud was born in France and moved to Dubai in 2007. She is an HR executive with 20-year experience in planning and implementing HR operations and strategy. In Dubai, she had several HR positions in global corporate banking and services industries being responsible for operational and strategic HR, advisory to management and boards, change and competence management programs and day-to-day admin delivery. Being a mum and a certified yoga teacher gives her a proactive and “feet on the ground’’ approach.

Bilbo Perrot

MANAGING DIRECTOR

Born in Geneva (Switzerland), Bilbo is a businessman of global vision and experience. Bilbo has a vast network thanks to his golf career which brought him to many different parts of the world: he was 11 times the Swiss National Golf Champion, he played for the Swiss National Team as well as for the European Continental Team. He also played 3 years on the Tour as professional. Bilbo also has several years in the educational sector of on boards and committees.

Christian Peter

CHIEF OPERATIONS OFFICER

Christian is specialised in corporate strategy and international investments for the previous 15 years, having carved out a career that has spanned across sectors of Real Estate, Hospitality, Agriculture, Retail and Media. With a truly global reach, that has seen Christian regularly undertake assignments in Africa, Central Europe, the Middle East and North America, he has been involved with investments valued in excess of US$ 7bn. Currently serving as a Director at EBC, his previous positions have included serving as the EGM for Kanoo Real Estate, on behalf of one of the largest multinational family offices in the GCC. Prior to this he was the Managing Director for The Birch Group Inc, a private equity company focused towards investments in real estate, retail, media and agriculture, and the Head of Strategy and Business Development for Rakeen, a government owned real estate investment and development company based in the United Arab Emirates.

Our Story

The English College, Dubai was established in 1992 and has grown into one of the most respected and highly sought-after British schools in Dubai and beyond. Initially established as a secondary school for Year 7 to Year 13 students only, 2005 saw the addition of The English College Primary school building for students from FS 1 to Year 6. Both being separate campuses initially, 2019 saw the merger of both the Primary and Secondary school to become an all-through Early Years to A-level British school, thus ensuring a smooth and seamless transition from FS 1 to Sixth Form.

In August 2020, our architecturally ambitious and facility rich secondary g + 3 building opened its doors, featuring 8 Chemistry/ Physics/ Science/Bio Labs; Prep Rooms; Graphics Studio; Art Studio; Music Class Room; Individual Music Practice Rooms; Group Music Practice Room; Black Box Theatre; Drama Studio Theatre; large Library; Media Studies Room; Learning Commons; Dining Hall with Terrace; Sixth Form Study Rooms; Sixth Form Common Room. The new PE/Sports Complex features: Fitness Studio; separate Changing Rooms for Female and Male; modern, full equipped Clinic for Nurse and part-time Doctor; Activity/PE Classroom and large PE/Sports Hall.

Following the British curriculum, all classes and lessons are taught and supported by our highly qualified and experienced teaching team. Our team regularly receive comprehensive professional development that contributes to our DSIB-rated “Very Good” Teaching and Learning. We have already embarked on the journey to make the transition from being rated among the very good British schools in Dubai to being among the outstanding British schools in Dubai.

Great emphasis is placed on developing the whole child in a stimulating, caring environment. Excellent and strong relationships exist between staff, student and parents – something we are very proud of.
